Our story

Catwok was formed by 4 ambitious best friends, who are passionate about cooking, eating, and traveling!

Our head chef, Fat Cat discovered his passion since the ending of his high school, so he quit university just to pursue the cooking dream. He’s been bringing yummy meals to the world for almost 15 years!
